About

Hi!

I'm originally from Ridgewood, NJ. I studied undergrad at New York University where I majored in both math and philosophy. After college, I stayed in NYC as a consultant for an additional year until I started my PhD at the University of Pennsylvania. I'm currently living in Philadelphia, PA.

Outside of philosophy, I enjoy reading and writing fiction, going for Sunday morning runs, playing a casual game of chess, and chatting with friends (or strangers!) over tea. Lastly, but maybe most importantly, I am also an ardent Swiftie.
